## <a name="spatial-coordinate-systems"></a><span data-ttu-id="38b4e-189">Sistemas de coordenadas espaciales</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38b4e-189">Spatial coordinate systems</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="38b4e-190">Usan todas las aplicaciones de gráficos 3D [sistemas de coordenadas cartesiano](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems) razonar sobre las posiciones y orientaciones de objetos en los mundos virtuales se representan.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38b4e-190">All 3D graphics applications use [Cartesian coordinate systems](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems) to reason about the positions and orientations of objects in the virtual worlds they render.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38b4e-191">Estos sistemas de coordenadas establecer 3 ejes perpendiculares a lo largo del que se va a colocar los objetos: un eje X, Y y Z.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38b4e-191">Such coordinate systems establish 3 perpendicular axes along which to position objects: an X, Y, and Z axis.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="38b4e-192">En [la realidad mixta](mixed-reality.md), las aplicaciones se razón sobre los sistemas de coordenadas virtuales y físicos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38b4e-192">In [mixed reality](mixed-reality.md), your apps will reason about both virtual and physical coordinate systems.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38b4e-193">Windows llama a un sistema de coordenadas que tiene un significado real en el mundo físico un **del sistema de coordenadas espacial**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38b4e-193">Windows calls a coordinate system that has real meaning in the physical world a **spatial coordinate system**.</span></span>
